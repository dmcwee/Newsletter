In addition to these themes we have identified some notable patterns in requests:
  • Many customers are migrating from or co‑existing with third‑party tools (especially CrowdStrike, Proofpoint) and want explicit mapping of capabilities and safe coexistence patterns.
  • A notable subset of engagements are sales or upsell‑adjacent (e.g., moving from E3 to E5/A5, MDE P1→P2, DSPM), using SME guidance to de‑risk adoption and drive license value realization.
  • Several requests explicitly require language or region‑specific SMEs (e.g., German, French, Korean, Japanese) and/or identity/security specialists, indicating demand for both technical depth and localized support

Spotlight
 
BVA/FT Partnership - AI-in-One Dashboard for ESS Agent Reporting
In December, Dentsu needed a way to analyse conversation transcripts and thumbs up/down feedback from their ESS pilot testers to improve topic quality and progress their deployment. The initial approach using Copilot Studio Kit proved challenging, documentation was scattered and the CSV output was difficult to interpret.
Vivian Ajetunmobi (BVA) and Georgi Nunev (FT SME) partnered with Keith McGrane from the Copilot Growth Analytics team to build a solution. Keith designed a new Agent Performance section in the AI-in-One Power BI dashboard, leveraging Copilot Studio transcript exports to enable deeper analysis of user proficiency and agent performance.
The dashboard is now published on GitHub and available for the field and customers. Following Keith's demo at the team call, 3 additional ESS customers (Northern Trust, Wells Fargo, FIS) were identified to pilot the dashboard alongside Dentsu.
A fantastic example of cross-team collaboration between BVA and FastTrack turning a customer challenge into a scalable solution.
